## Overview

MariaDB is a community-developed, commercially supported fork of the MySQL relational database management system. It is designed to be a drop-in replacement for MySQL, offering the same performance, scalability, and features.

MariaDB is a high-performance, enterprise-grade relational database management system that provides a robust and reliable foundation for building and running complex applications. It is designed to be scalable, reliable, and secure, with a focus on performance and ease of use.

## Connecting

To connect to MariaDB, follow these general steps:

1. **Open the DBCode Extension**: Launch Visual Studio Code and open the DBCode extension.
2. **Add a New Connection**: Click on the "Add Connection" icon.
3. **Complete new connection form**: Choose MariaDB as the type, and enter the required information.
4. **Connect**: Click save to connect to your MariaDB database.
5. **Start Managing Your Databases**: Once connected, you can start managing your databases directly from Visual Studio Code.

For detailed instructions on connecting to MariaDB, refer to the [Connect](/docs/get-started/connect) article.

## Connect Multiple Databases	

A number of cloud providers offer MariaDB as a service. To connect to a cloud provider and access multiple databases, follow these general steps:

1. **Open the DBCode Extension**: Launch Visual Studio Code and open the DBCode extension.
2. **Add a New Connection**: Click on the "Add Connection" icon.
3. **Select Your Provider**: Choose the cloud provider from the list on the right.
4. **Authenticate**: Follow the authentication process specific to the provider.
5. **Start Managing Your Databases**: Once connected, you can start managing your databases directly from Visual Studio Code.

For detailed instructions on connecting to MariaDB, refer to the [Connect a Cloud Provider](/docs/cloud-providers/connect) article.

By using MariaDB with DBCode, you can connect to your MariaDB databases, query and manage your data, and visualize your results, all directly from Visual Studio Code.
